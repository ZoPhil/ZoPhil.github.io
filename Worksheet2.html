What are these?

All of the art you see here is from the Kunsthalle in Hamburg in Germany. This is a great museum to vist to see a wide variety of art.


Anything else?

Of course, you don't only need a museum to enjoy art. There are a ton of artists still alive and doing wonderful new things. And you can even create your own art just like these old masters.



Paintings

As you may have noticed, a lot of the art featured here are paintings. A lot of the art that seems to have survived over the centuries, and especially those that have found their way into museums, are paintings. Perhaps this is due to their size. They are generally flat and stackable and therfore can be displayed and stored without taking up too much space.

Sculptures

There are, of course, many other kinds art, especially 3-D art such as sculptures. There were, in fact, some sculptures in the Kunsthalle, howvever not nearly as many as the paintings. This may be becuase they are generally harder to transport and store becuase of their generally larger size and stranger shapes that risk parts of the pice being broken.
