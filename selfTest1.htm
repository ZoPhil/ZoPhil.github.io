Race

The first thing you have to do is pick a race. Becuase this is a world of fantasy and possiblilities your character isn't restricted to just these homogenous human bodies. Instead, you character can be large or small or scaly or smooth or have wings or fangs or claws or horns. You can breathe air or water or fire or all of the above! Of course, all of these decsions can be made using careful thought and consideration. However, this is a quickest build in exisitance. Therefore, it is all random!
